TimeQuest Concepts and Terminology

Last updated May 29, 2011

Setup Time Slack

Data must arrive at time t2, ahead of the clock rising edge, to satisfy the setup time. The data actually arrive early at time t1, and the earliness of the (t2 - t1) gap is the setup time slack.

 

Hold Time Slack

Data must remain until time t3, after the clock edge has risen, to satisfy the hold time. The next piece of data actually arrive late at time t4. The lateness of the (t4 - t3) gap is the hold time slack.

 

Terminology

Term Definition
Cell Device building blocks (look up tables, multipliers, etc.)
Pin Input or output of cells
Net Connection between pins
Port Top-level inputs and outputs

An I/O element is both a cell (since it's a device building block) and a port (since it's a top level input / output).
