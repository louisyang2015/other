Math2Html updates:

09/17 --- "sub" and "exp" no longer add new lines after formatting; previously "_sub(a)_b" will create a space between "a" and "b" because of the new line, now it won't

09/17 --- the limits of integration in "_integral()_" is no longer aligned to the center of the cell.
