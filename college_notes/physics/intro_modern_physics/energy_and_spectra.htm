The Energy and Spectra of Molecules

The total energy of the molecule:

E = Ee1 + Etrans + Erot + Evib

Ee1 --- electronic energy
Etrans --- translational energy as the molecule's center of mass slides through space
Erot --- rotational energy as the molecule spins on its center of mass
Evib --- vibrational energy due to the molecule's vibrating atoms.

This section talks about the last two forms of energy.
