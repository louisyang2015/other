Induced Charges

An object that is neutral overall can have induced charges.

If a suspended object A is attracted to object B, we can not conclude that object A is charged. It's is possible that object A has induced charges.

Electric Field Lines

This is a way to visualize electric fields.

The lines begin on positive charges and terminate on negative charges. If the net charge is not zero, lines may begin or terminate at infinity.

The number of lines drawn leaving a positive charge or approaching a negative charge is proportional to the magnitude of the charge.

No two field lines can cross or touch.
