The Ideal Transformer

How an ideal transformer works is a physics issue --- this documentation only deals with the circuit issues.

Ideal Transformer Basic Equations:

Note that i2(t) is going in the opposite of the direction implied by V2(t) --- this is done to result in simpler equations. Other than the i2(t), the orientation is exactly the same as the mutual inductance case.

These are the basic laws of the transformer and is all that I used to do the problems for this section. Make nodal or mesh equations for each side of the transformer, and then relate the two sides with the above equations. The rest of this section gives additional insight, but is not essential.
